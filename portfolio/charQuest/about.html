About the Site

This site is the answer to what every new player asks: What does any of this actually mean? Dungeons and Dragons is more user-friendly than every with the advent of Fifth Edition, but at the end of the day there are a lot of terms and basics which experienced players and dungeon masters take for granted. This site aims to answer all of the above in a friendly, understandable fashion.

About the Author

I'm a student, a web designer, a D&D player, and a Dungeon Master. I've been playing D&D for as long as I can remember, and have played every edition except for 3.5 (because sometimes self-care is opting out of a broken system). I built this site over a couple of months, with several complete teardowns and rebuilds. My hope is that it will serve as a useful resource in the future.
